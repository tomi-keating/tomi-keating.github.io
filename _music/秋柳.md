堤边柳 到秋天 叶乱飘
Willow trees on the river bank
In autumn days
Their leaves flutter

叶落尽 只剩得 细枝条
The leaves are all gone
Only bare twigs are left

想当日 绿茵茵 春光好
Recall those days
Green were grass
Warm was spring

今日里 冷清清 秋色老
But today
Cold is wind
Fade is autumn

风凄凄 雨凄凄
Wind, sad
Rain, sad

君不见 眼前景 已全非
Don't you see
The days were gone

眼前景 已全非
The days were gone

一思量 一回首 不胜悲
When I recall
When I look back
I feel nothing but sadness.
